# 🧵 Threads To Trends

## AI-Powered Fashion Business Intelligence Platform

Threads To Trends is an AI-powered Business Intelligence platform that analyzes Google Maps business data to identify high-potential fashion boutiques with limited digital presence. The platform transforms raw business data into actionable insights using data analytics, feature engineering, opportunity scoring, location intelligence, and AI-driven recommendations.

The project helps identify businesses that have strong customer trust but weak online visibility, enabling strategic digital transformation through interactive dashboards and executive reports.

## 🏗 Architecture Overview

The application follows a modular Business Intelligence architecture. Raw Google Maps business data is collected and cleaned before passing through a feature engineering pipeline where Digital Score, Opportunity Score, Business Segmentation, and Growth Priority are calculated. The processed dataset is then consumed by a Flask backend that exposes REST APIs to an interactive dashboard. The dashboard provides executive analytics, digital gap analysis, opportunity identification, location intelligence, and AI recommendations. Finally, the platform generates professional PDF audit reports and PowerPoint presentations for business decision-making.
